## Suggested Sources:

- **Freesound.org** - Creative Commons ambient sounds
- **Splice** - Royalty-free samples
- **Generate your own** using synthesis tools like:
  - Audacity (tone generators, noise)
  - VCV Rack (modular synthesis)
  - Pure Data / Max/MSP
  
## Audio Specifications:

- Format: MP3
- Length: 5-15 seconds recommended
- Loop-friendly preferred
- Mono or stereo both work
- Bitrate: 128kbps minimum

## Note:

Audio files are **not included** in this repository due to licensing. You must source or create your own ambient sounds that match the thematic descriptions above.
